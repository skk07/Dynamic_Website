    About Us

    Shreevidya Internet Café is one of the leading and providing consistently Internet café services in Solapur since 1999.

    Shreevidya Internet cafe provides fast internet facility amongst the people of Solapur, an insatiable need for access to internet has spread out across all societies in Solapur and nearby areas.

    People have arrived at a conclusion that implies that existing internet cafes are providing shoddy services; hence it is the prerogative of Shreevidya Internet cafe to provide quality services that clients deserve. To complement this, the cafe will provide, in addition to internet surfing, photocopying, printing, typing, laminating, scanning, online applications, form filling and other online services to make it a one window shop.

    Keys to success

    The internet cafe will be a great success because there are very few internet cafes which provide a one-stop shop, (exact number remains unknown) in the area and therefore the Shreevidya Internet Cafe will have its own monopoly as far as provision of high profile service is concerned.

    Missions

    The mission of our internet-cafe is to make available internet service for greater society. In such a way that people will have access to all the sources which can be found on the internet. Thereby the cafe is supposed to be a place where people of all ages will come to enjoy the unique, upscale, educational, and innovative and safety environment that the internet-cafe provides.
